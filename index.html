This is a social experiment to figure out how people are talking on twitter right now.


This application fetches LIVE tweets from twitter stream and depending upon the words in the tweet decides if a tweet belongs to 'kind' or 'crude' category.


● Kind Words :
love, happy, beautiful
● Crude Words :
f__k, s__t, b___h
